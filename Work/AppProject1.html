Project description

Project description

My client, the owner of Green Line, a company that sells IFFCO’s Adblue Solution— a non-toxic, non-hazardous, and non-flammable diesel exhaust fluid made from high-quality Urea Solution approached me for a custom GST billing management software. Their goal was to streamline the billing process and ensure compliance with GST regulations

Timeline

From explorations to final designs in 5 weeks while working with multiple projects at the same time

Process

Process

Process

This category details the step-by-step approach taken during the project, including research, planning, design, development, testing, and optimization phases.

Research & Planning

Conducted market research to pinpoint current billing management challenges faced by clients in the agricultural sector. Identified target audience segments and outlined essential features based on user needs and industry trends, ensuring that the billing management app effectively addresses real-world requirements

Design & Prototyping

Collaborated with designers to create intuitive user interfaces and interactive prototypes. Iteratively refined designs based on my client's feedback to enhance usability and visual appeal.

Development & Implementation

Utilized agile development methodologies to create the billing management app for Green Line. Focused on prioritizing feature development based on client feedback and technical feasibility. Integrated advanced algorithms to analyze billing patterns and optimize financial recommendations for clients.

Testing & Optimization

Conducted rigorous testing across various devices and platforms to ensure compatibility and performance. Gathered user feedback through beta testing and iteratively optimized the app based on usability metrics and user satisfaction.

Solution

Solution

Solution

Green-line is an Android application for bill management and reporting. It allows users to generate bills, manage purchase details, and generate reports for their business needs as well as you can get Invoice as pdf.

Bill Generation

Create bills with customer information, item details, pricing, and taxes

Purchase Management

Track purchase details including supplier information, item descriptions, and payment records.

Reporting

Generate reports based on sales, purchases, or custom criteria.

Edit bills/ delete

Admin can Delete / Edit bills after generation

Share Bills

Share bills directly through whatsapp

View Generated Bills

view Generated bills within the app

Information about Generator

Get information about who is generated that bill in bills section

Profile Management

Update and manage user profile information.

  • Client Details section

    If an existing client is selected, all information will be auto-filled. Users can also create a new client, making the process seamless and efficient.
